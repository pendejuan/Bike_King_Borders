Biking Trails | Intermediate

Take your skills to the next level on the intermediate trail, designed for intermediate riders looking for a challenge. This 5-mile loop features a mix of smooth singletrack, moderate climbs, and fun descents, with an elevation gain of 300 feet. You'll navigate through a variety of terrain, including rocky sections, tight switchbacks, and scenic overlooks. With a few obstacles to test your skills, including small jumps and log crossings, this trail is perfect for riders looking to push themselves and improve their technique!
