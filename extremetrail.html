Biking Trails | Extreme

Get ready to unleash your inner daredevil on the Extreme trail, designed for expert riders who crave the ultimate adrenaline rush! This intense 7-mile loop features steep, technical climbs, heart-pounding descents, and treacherous obstacles that will push your skills to the limit. With an elevation gain of 1,000 feet, you'll need to be in top physical condition to tackle the grueling terrain, which includes massive jumps, rocky chutes, and treacherous drop-offs. The trail's narrow, exposed sections and precipitous cliffside routes will test your nerve and focus. The Extreme trail is for experienced riders who live for the thrill of extreme mountain biking.
